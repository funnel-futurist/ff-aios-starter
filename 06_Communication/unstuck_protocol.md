# Unstuck Protocol

> When you're stuck, don't stay stuck. Use this decision tree to get moving in under 60 seconds.

---

## The Core Rule

**There should never be a reason to stay stuck.** You have AI tools that are infinitely patient, never judge you, and will explain the same thing 47 different ways if that's what you need.

---

## Step 1: Pick Your Tool

| If you need to... | Use this |
|---|---|
| **DO** something (click buttons, fix bugs, navigate UI) | **AI Studio Live** — share your screen + talk through it |
| **KNOW** something (plan, write, draft, understand) | **Claude Code** in this workspace (or Gemini chat) |
| **RESEARCH** something (explore options before starting) | **Gemini Deep Research** — gather context first |

**Quick links:**
- [Gemini (Chat + Code)](https://gemini.google.com)
- [AI Studio (Live Voice + Screen)](https://aistudio.google.com)
- Claude Code: already open in this workspace

---

## Step 2: Use These Prompts

### "I Don't Know How to Start"

> "I am trying to **[your goal]**, but I don't know the first step. Treat me like a beginner. Give me a numbered list of the major phases, then walk me through Step 1 in extreme detail. Tell me exactly what to click."

### "Something Broke"

> "I expected **[what should have happened]** but **[what actually happened]**. I've attached a screenshot of the error. Explain why this is happening and tell me exactly how to fix it, step-by-step."

### "Explain Like I'm 5"

> "I'm reading this documentation **[paste text]** but I don't understand it. Please rewrite this into a simple checklist I can follow. Remove the jargon and just tell me the actions I need to take."

### "I'm Totally Overwhelmed"

> "I have no idea what I should be doing. Help me extract what I should be focused on by asking me questions. Turn my answers into a clear, simple action list I can execute without getting stuck."

---

## Step 3: The One-Step Rule

1. Execute **only** the first step AI gives you
2. Did it work? Reply: *"Done. Next."*
3. Did it fail? Screenshot it and reply: *"This didn't work. See image."*
4. Repeat until done

**Don't read paragraphs.** Scan for **bold text** and **action verbs**.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Fix |
|---|---|
| AI is going too fast | Reply: *"Stop. Break that last step into 3 smaller micro-steps."* |
| Can't find a button | Reply: *"I don't see that button. Look at this screenshot."* |
| AI is wrong/hallucinating | Switch to **AI Studio Live** and talk through it with screen sharing |
| Still stuck after 15 minutes | **Escalate:** Post in your Slack channel or book a Technical Support Call |

---

## When to Escalate to a Human

- You've tried AI help for 15+ minutes with no progress
- The issue involves account access, billing, or credentials
- Something is broken that AI can't see (server-side, platform-level)
- You need a strategic decision, not a technical fix

**Escalation path:**
1. Post in your Slack channel with a screenshot + what you tried
2. If urgent: book a Technical Support Call or AIOS Hot Seat
