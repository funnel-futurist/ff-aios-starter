# Support Touchpoints — Your Human + AI Support System

> You're never alone in this. Here's every way you can get help, when to use each one, and how to book them.

---

## Your Support Layers

### Layer 1: AI Support (Available 24/7)

| Tool | When to Use | How |
|---|---|---|
| **Claude Code (this workspace)** | Questions about your strategy, offer, brand, or any docs | Just type your question |
| **AI Coach (Portal)** | Quick questions when browsing the portal | Chat widget at success.funnelfuturist.com |
| **Unstuck Protocol** | When you're stuck and not sure which tool to use | Type: "Open the unstuck protocol" |

**Use AI first for:** Quick questions, drafting content, reviewing copy, understanding your docs, brainstorming, finding information.

---

### Layer 2: Scheduled Calls (Book as Needed)

| Call Type | What It's For | Duration | How to Book |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Catalyst Call** | Strategic check-in — review progress, set direction, remove roadblocks, celebrate wins | 30 min | Through your portal or ask your agency contact |
| **Technical Support Call** | Specific tech issue — workspace setup, platform questions, tool troubleshooting | 15 min | Through your portal or Slack |
| **AIOS Hot Seat** | Deep dive on your AI workspace — skills, prompts, workflow optimization | 30 min | Through your portal |
| **QBR (Quarterly Business Review)** | Big picture — metrics, strategy pivot assessment, next quarter planning | 45-60 min | Scheduled by agency quarterly |

**When to book a call vs. ask AI:**
- AI can't see your screen → book a Technical Support Call
- You need a strategic decision, not information → book a Catalyst Call
- You want to learn new workspace skills → book a Hot Seat
- Everything else → ask Claude first

---

### Layer 3: Async Communication (Anytime)

| Channel | When to Use | Response Time |
|---|---|---|
| **Slack** (if set up) | Quick questions, sharing updates, non-urgent issues | Same day |
| **Email** | Formal requests, document sharing, scheduling | 24-48 hours |
| **Review queue feedback** | Deliverable revisions (tell Claude "I don't like X") | Logged instantly, agency acts within 48hrs |
| **GitHub Issues** (advanced) | Feature requests or bug reports on your workspace | Next business day |

---

## The Escalation Path

```
1. Ask Claude (instant)
   ↓ still stuck after 5 min?
2. Check the unstuck protocol (2 min)
   ↓ still stuck?
3. Message on Slack or email (same day response)
   ↓ need real-time help?
4. Book a Technical Support Call (15 min, next available)
   ↓ strategic, not technical?
5. Book a Catalyst Call (30 min)
```

## Call Rhythm by Phase

| Phase | Typical Call Cadence | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Foundation** | Weekly or biweekly | F6 review, offer refinement, setup |
| **Cyborg VA & AIOS** | Biweekly | VA placement, workspace optimization |
| **CRM & Monetization** | Biweekly | Pipeline setup, sequence review |
| **Distribution** | Monthly | Content review, ad performance |
| **Ongoing Success** | Monthly + Quarterly QBR | Metrics, strategy, scaling |

Your cadence adjusts based on how active you are. More activity = more frequent touchpoints. Coasting = monthly check-ins to keep momentum.
